Project 2:

Store Management System(Fall, 2021)

Project Description:

Store management ensures that various activities that are involved in maintaining the store and controlling the stock should be carried out efficiently. Maintenance of proper records of new, stored and sold items is one of the important functions of stores with which a proper database is maintained. Consumers spend a lot of money every year on purchase of items in the stores as their basic needs. Retail store owners who provide services to their customers should have a stable and reliable database to give efficient and quality of service. This will automatically give lifelong customers for the stores. With this database, it ensures better supervision and control over items and drastically increases the productivity of the store.

Implemented: Entity Relational model.
Relational Scheme.
Several SQL queries for Store Management System.
Language Used : SQL

Bachelor’s Major Project (2019):

Title: An Adaptive and Parallel Non-Dominated Sorting Genetic Algorithm to solve Multi-objective Flexible Job Shop Scheduling.

Project Description:

Developed an evolutionary algorithm by integrating Non-Dominated Sorting Genetic Algorithm (NSGA-II) with Adaptive and Parallel features(ANSGA-II) to solve Multi-Objective Job shop scheduling optimization problems. On over 10 BRdataset instances compared to traditional genetic algorithm, developed ANSGA-II performance has been increased by 62% faster

Language used: Java
